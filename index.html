INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS

  1. CONNECT YOUR ESP32 CYD

    Connect the device to your computer using a USB cable. Make sure the drivers are installed.

  2. CLICK "CONNECT & FLASH"

    The browser will ask you to select the correct COM port for your ESP32.

  3. SELECT THE PORT

    Look for something like "USB Serial Port" or "CP2102" in the list and select it.

  4. INSTALL THE FIRMWARE

    Click "Install" and wait for the process to finish (may take 1-2 minutes).

  5. DONE!

    Once completed, the ESP32 will automatically restart with the new firmware.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT BROWSERS ARE COMPATIBLE?

Chrome, Edge, and Opera in their latest versions. Firefox and Safari do not yet support Web Serial API.

DO I NEED TO INSTALL DRIVERS?

On Windows, you may need to install CH340 or CP2102 drivers depending on your ESP32's USB chip.

WILL THE PROCESS ERASE MY DATA?

Yes, flashing will overwrite all content in the ESP32's flash memory.

WHAT DO I DO IF INSTALLATION FAILS?

Try holding down the BOOT button on the ESP32 while connecting USB, then release and try again.
